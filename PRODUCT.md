# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Small contractor business owners and operations managers. They use this as their primary command center to track daily jobs, manage invoicing cycles, and monitor client health. The context is often high-pressure or multitasking.

## Product Purpose
To provide a minimalist, lightning-fast operations dashboard that removes the complexity of traditional CRM/ERP systems for small trade businesses. Success is measured by "time to action"—how quickly a user can identify and resolve an outstanding task.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Functional, High-Contrast. It feels like a professional tool, not a social app.

## Anti-references
- Soft, "bubbly" SaaS UI with heavy shadows and pastels.
- Card-in-card nesting.
- Gratuitous animations that delay data display.
- Decorative "hero" metrics with no actionable context.

## Design Principles
1. **Data over Decoration**: Visual elements (borders, colors) are only used to signify status or hierarchy, never for style alone.
2. **Action-Oriented Density**: Maintain a high information density that favors utility over white space.
3. **Rigid Geometry**: Stick to a strict 8px grid with consistent border radii (8px) and no depth (no shadows).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High color contrast for status badges.
- Large, legible hit targets for row actions.
- Keyboard-navigable tables.
